About us

Visualize Air Pollution is a project developed by the University of Memphis.
Our mission is to visualize the air pollution levels and potential sources on Google Earth.
This project will promote the public's awareness of air pollution, help them to identify potential pollution sources, and support the environment justice (EJ) movement.
